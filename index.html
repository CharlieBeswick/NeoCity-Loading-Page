What is NeoCity?

NeoCity is a next-generation Garry's Mod city roleplay gamemode built from the ground up with modern systems and custom mechanics. Our goal is to create a living, breathing city where every interaction feels immersive and adrenaline-packed. Weapons and ammo are expensive and hard to buy, and a player can kill you for them if he catches you slipping.

Core Features

  • ✓ Persistent economy & banking — Earn, spend, save, and build wealth across play sessions
  • ✓ Safe zones (WIP)
  • ✓ Faction system with dynamic growth — Players and NPCs belong to evolving factions
  • ✓ Body looting — Kill players to loot them
  • ✓ Custom NPCs & AI behavior — Advanced NPCs powered by bespoke logic
  • ✓ Deep inventory & item systems — Modular inventory, holsters, containers, lootables
  • ✓ Player-driven city development — Businesses, crime, trading all feed into city growth
  • ✓ Modern UI & immersive design — Clean menus, polished HUDs, sci-fi visual identity

The Vision

NeoCity isn't just another RP server — it's a lawless, dog-eats-dog dystopian hellhole. Or its heaven, depends on the player. There are no police... unless you want to be a vigilante? Be nice to others and they will be nice to you. Rob others and you won't stand a chance.
